Pyro Central

    Pyrography is an art where you decorate wood or leather by burning it. I learned about it when I went on a girl scout camping trip. My mother brought the tool and I fell in love with it instantly. Since then I have enjoyed pyrography. Later I began burning cedar because of it's nice pink color and the scent when it burns is wonderful.

    There are some problems with the art of pyrography like:

    1. Smoke in your eyes and nose
    2. Constantly burning yourself
    3. Spinters in your fingers
    4. Wood shreddings in your eyes

    These are just some of the consequences of wood burning or pyrography. Even though some may say that this is dangerous I think it's fun and completly worth the burns. Nothing beats the feeling of completing a new peice.
